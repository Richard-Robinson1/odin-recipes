Mac and Cheese

Simple but delicious Mac and Cheese

This is a very basic macaroni and cheese that is good on it's own, or can be used as the base to many different spins.

Ingredients

  • 1lb elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk, warmed
  • 1-2lb sharp cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil and drain macaroni per box instructions.
  2. Melt the butter in a skillet.
  3. Sprinkle in flour and stir until pasty.
  4. Whisk in warmed milk and let cook until thickened.
  5. Add cheese to sauce.
  6. Add sauce to pasta.
